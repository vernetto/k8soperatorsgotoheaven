# Kubernetes Operators Go To Heaven

## Season 1


# Episode 1

## Title
Placeholder title for episode 1

## Opening Scene

03:17 AM. Ahmed receives an emergency call.

Production is failing.

Nobody knows why.

## Investigation

Ahmed starts with:

```bash
kubectl get pods -A
```

He gathers evidence, interviews engineers, and follows the clues.

## Technical Lesson

This episode teaches a real Kubernetes troubleshooting technique.

## Resolution

Ahmed identifies the root cause and restores production.

## Lessons Learned

- Observe before changing.
- Gather evidence.
- Verify assumptions.
- Fix the root cause.
